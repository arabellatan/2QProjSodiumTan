TF2 Comics

Comics for Team Fortress 2 were initially made to promote new updates for the game. The Team Fortress Comics series was later made, which expanded on the classes' stories and introduced the mercenaries from Team Fortress Classic.

Descriptions of each comic can be found at https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Comics, and all comics (excluding the non-canon Death of a Salesbot) can be read directly from https://www.teamfortress.com/comics.php or by clicking on the images below.
